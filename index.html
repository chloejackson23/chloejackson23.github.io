I am a second year undergraduate student at the University of California, Riverside pursuing a degree in Theatre, Film, and Digital Production with a focus in Film making and Producing or Directing. Although I do not have any professional hands-on experience in this field as of yet, my school provides a number of opportunities and resources to get started in making connections, getting experience through plays and productions and more. I have also already begun creating a network through my mutuals in order to reach out and shadow professionals in this career path who can help me get where I want to be.

Growing up loving cinema and everything surrounding it, including the experience of going to the movies or even creating iMovie shorts with my friends for fun, I have always had an interest in the film making realm in some way. Whether it was film editing, being the camera operator, the CGI tech, or even directing, I have always found myself yearning to be a part of the movie magic. I find it so intriguing how immersive of an experience it can be to simply watch a body of work. The behind the scenes versus what we see on screen is so incredible to me regarding how human beings can create such powerful and moving films and it all be fake. Even films that are not completely fake like reenactments of true events or documentaries have inspired me to get involved with the world of storytelling.

As I go through my college journey, I have become interested in learning more about technology, including computer science. I feel like having knowledge in this area can even possibly be beneficial for the career path I am looking forward to getting into as our world becomes much more digital. Computer scientists do a multitude of things that are maybe overlooked or seen as something that just "happens" when in reality they are who make it happen. I want to be one of the people that makes it happen. Therefore, I look forward to learning much more in this world and being able to apply it not only on it own but in the film making world as well.
